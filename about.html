About

Creating a user interface is not done in one step. The best practices involve a process of researching, designing, sketching, prototyping, testing, and redesigning before writing any code.

In this collection of projects that I share with you, I explore all of these different steps in the design process using a number of different design tools. With every project, I learn more about the user experience!

Take a look through each of my projects too see what I learned, what I struggled with, and how I'm going to improve moving forward.

Other than designing and coding, I love playing the piano, playing video games, painting, drawing, reading, and improving my typing speed on monkeytype.com. (162 WPM!)
